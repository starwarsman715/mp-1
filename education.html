Education

  • Liceo Italiano Madrid:

    Liceo Italiano Madrid is a well-regarded Italian international school located in Madrid. It offers a curriculum based on the Italian education system, providing a multicultural environment where students can receive a bilingual education in both Italian and Spanish. The school emphasizes academic excellence while fostering a strong sense of community and cultural diversity.

  • Boston University:

    Boston University (BU) is a prestigious private research university located in Boston, Massachusetts. Known for its academic rigor and diverse range of programs, BU offers a vibrant campus life and is home to a global community of students. With a strong emphasis on interdisciplinary learning and innovation, the university fosters a dynamic and inclusive environment, preparing students for leadership roles in various fields.
